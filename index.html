What it does

The work of a lean marketing team, running as software.

Masthead is being built as a managed operator first: safer for pilots, sharper for learning, and easier to sell before self-serve SaaS.

01

Reads the store

Catalog, stock, product priority, margins, blocked topics and brand voice live under one client profile.

02

Chooses the angle

Product spotlight, category guide, campaign offer, evergreen tips or news reaction. Not every business is a news feed.

03

Creates the post

Carousel structure, caption, CTA, image search and visual checks are generated from a structured brief.

04

Routes approval

Human approval stays in the loop before anything goes live. Pilots stay safe by default.

05

Learns what works

Every run becomes structured evidence for the next creative, campaign and store decision.

Why this matters

Most e-commerce stores do not need more tools. They need execution.

Agencies are expensive and slow.

Routine marketing work should not cost thousands a month before a store even knows what angle will work.

AI content without inventory context is shallow.

The product direction is to connect stock, margins and campaign opportunities so content follows business reality.

The wedge is managed service, then software.

Win pilots manually, learn from every run, then harden the repeatable pieces into the platform.

Roadmap

From content engine to marketing operating system.

The live prototype already handles clients, product-aware briefs, approval workflow and Meta onboarding foundations.

Phase 1

Inventory-aware content

Shopify and Shoptet catalogs, promotable product selection, stock-safe posts, catalog image provenance and client review.

Phase 2

Meta campaign loop

Approved creatives become ads, budgets stay capped, results come back into the reporting model, and winners inform the next run.

Phase 3

Cross-store learning

Structured creative metadata makes Masthead smarter across verticals without exposing tenant data.
