Roadmap

From content engine to a marketing operating system.

Phase 1

Plugged into your store.

Connects to Shopify & Shoptet, reads live inventory, builds and publishes campaigns for what's overstocked — and stops promoting what's sold out.

Phase 2

Closed-loop performance marketing.

Deploys content as Meta ads, splits the daily budget, A/B tests, and moves spend to the winners automatically. The owner never lifts a finger.

Phase 3

A data network across every store.

With 1,000+ stores, Masthead learns across all of them — a winning format in one market is applied to others before competitors notice.
